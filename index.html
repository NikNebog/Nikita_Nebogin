Essay

Lightning is a professional racer in the Piston Cup racing series, the real prototype is the NASCAR Cup Series. He won 7 cups in 2006-2016. In Cars 2, he participates in the World Grand Prix, an event promoting a new fuel called Allinol. At the end of Wheelbarrows 3, he briefly assumes the role of chief mechanic and mentor to Cruz Ramirez before returning to racing.
